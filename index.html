About Me

Hi, I'm Ian.

I'm currently a first-year radiology resident at Brigham and Women's Hospital in Boston, Massachusetts.

My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, data science, and healthcare. I believe we can integrate AI into medicine to make life easier for doctors and better for patients. My expertise is primarily in applied deep learning and computer vision.

I'm an avid Kaggle competitor and the world's first Kaggle Grandmaster-doctor combo. I've won over $50,000 in prize money from competitions.

In my free time, I like watching K-dramas, videos about food, taking naps, and eating ice cream.

I was born in Urbana, Illinois and grew up in the suburbs of Los Angeles, California. I spent 8 years in Rhode Island for school and now consider myself a proud New Englander.
